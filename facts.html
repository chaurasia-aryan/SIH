Facts About Water Conservation

  • About 71% of the Earth's surface is water-covered, but only a small fraction is available for human use.
  • Using a broom instead of a hose to clean driveways and sidewalks saves water.
  • Fixing leaks can save over 10,000 gallons of water per year in the average household.

Join the effort to save water! Every drop counts.
